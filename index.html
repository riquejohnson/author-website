The Author

Rique Johnson

Rique Johnson is an author of mystery and contemporary fiction, published by Simon & Schuster. His novels weave together suspense, romance, and moral complexity to create stories that stay with readers long after the final page.

From the gritty streets of Virginia to the tangled emotions of the human heart, Johnson's work explores what it means to love fiercely, fight for justice, and survive the consequences of both. His writing has earned a devoted readership drawn to his authentic voice and relentless storytelling.

A prolific novelist with six published works, Johnson continues to craft fiction that challenges, moves, and captivates. His imagination comes across as creative, bold, and sometimes edgy. Rique is often called a storyteller. He writes so that the readers can place themselves into the pages of the story and make the pages play like a movie in their own imagination. He is a passionate writer who is unafraid to reveal the sensitivity of a male or himself, thus, invoking an emotional response from the readers.

Scent

People often say that we should take time to smell the roses.

Take a moment to enjoy the simplest things in life.

I have inhaled deeply.

I became captivated by your scent.

Not by the fragrance of your perfume.

Not by the smell of your shampoo.

Simply, captivated by the aroma within you.

I smell a rose every time the thought of you dances in my mind.

I smell a rose every time I'm treated to the brilliance of your smile.

I smell a rose every time the pleasing vibrations of your voice touch my ears.

I smell a rose every time our glances become a hidden embrace.

Your presence is like walking through a field of flowers.

Vibrant and stimulating.

Bathing me with your aura.

Touching me within.

I have inhaled deeply.

Your scent could last a lifetime.

— Rique Johnson
